About Me

Hello, my name is Arvin Satya Pratama. I am a graphic designer with experience in the creative industry. I am interested in a wide variety of design projects, including poster, brochure, business card, clothing and social media design. I am also deepening my skills to create a website and the website you are currently visiting is made by me personally. I have the ability to communicate well with clients to understand their needs and produce designs that match their vision. I have worked with a wide variety of clients from all walks of life. Some of the clients I've worked with include food companies, technology companies, clothing brands, creative movements and band merchandise. I am very proud of the quality of my work and always try to give the best results. I continue to develop my skills by taking online courses. I am skilled in using various design tools such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, Canva and Figma. I really enjoy what I do and feel very lucky to be working in a field that I love. I believe that every design project is an opportunity to create something beautiful and useful. I am happy to see my clients are satisfied with my work and look forward to working with you in the future. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, I look forward to working with you.
